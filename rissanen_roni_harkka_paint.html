22.4.2020

Mickey's inner beauty

I usually start to hate my drawings quite quick but this piece from 2016 still holds up. I think it was originally done for high school art class. At least I remember submitting it to get the course done.

The idea behind this one was to mix the maskot of Iron Maiden and Mickey Mouse. At the time I was experimenting with punching holes through my drawings and putting photos in the created space. I ended up using a photo of a rose for this one which made it a lot gruesome than at first intended.

Lines were done in paint and the photograph was added in the background using GIMP.
